Advanced Analysis Methodology

  • Flexible Metric Selection: Choose to analyze Productivity Loss, Excess Fuel Used, or CO₂ Emissions individually
  • Multiple Emission Models: Select Basic, Barth's, or MOVES formulas for precise calculations
  • Vehicle-Specific Parameters: Tailored fuel consumption and emission factors for each vehicle class
  • Advanced Parameters: Incorporates stops per km, road grade, and temperature for accurate analysis
  • Economic Impact: Quantifies fuel costs and productivity losses for comprehensive insights
  • Environmental Focus: Calculates CO₂ emissions with industry-standard factors

How It Works

  1. Configure Analysis: Select your desired metric, emission model, and state to tailor your analysis
  2. Data Collection: Input traffic data for vehicle classes across selected cities in the chosen state
  3. Analysis: View detailed metrics and visualizations in the generated dashboard
  4. Reporting: Download CSV or PDF reports for actionable insights
  5. Decision Support: Leverage data-driven insights for effective transportation planning
